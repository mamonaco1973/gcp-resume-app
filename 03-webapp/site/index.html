How to Use My Jobs

Getting Started
  1. Sign in or create an account.
  2. Click the document icon to add your resume — paste your resume text and give it a name. You can store multiple resumes.
  3. Click the + icon to score a new job. Choose a source:
    • Job URL — paste any public job posting link.
    • Paste Job Description — paste raw text directly.
    • LinkedIn Job IDs — paste one or more numeric job IDs.
  4. Submit — the AI scores your resume against the job in the background. Results appear within seconds.
Understanding Scores

Each job is scored 0–100 based on how well your resume matches the job requirements.

90–100Exceptional match — nearly all requirements met
70–89Strong match — most requirements met, minor gaps
50–69Moderate match — some relevant experience, notable gaps
30–49Weak match — limited relevant experience, significant gaps
0–29Poor match — little to no relevant experience

Open a job to see the full AI analysis — strengths, weaknesses, and a written summary.

Folders
  • Use folders to organize jobs by stage, company, or any category you choose.
  • Create a folder with the folder+ icon in the filter bar.
  • Assign a job to a folder at submission time, or move jobs in bulk by selecting rows and using the Move to control.
  • Filter the table to a specific folder using the Folder dropdown.
Attachments
  • Attach files to any job — offer letters, notes, interview prep docs, etc.
  • Up to 5 files per job, 10 MB per file.
  • Supported types: PDF, Word, Excel, images, text, ZIP.
  • Open a job and expand the Attachments section to upload or download files.
  • A paperclip icon appears in the table for jobs that have attachments — click it to download directly from the dashboard.
Token Usage

Each job scoring request consumes AI tokens. Your usage is shown as a ring in the filter bar.

  • The ring fills as you use your budget — it turns red at 80%.
  • When your budget is exhausted, new job submissions are blocked.
